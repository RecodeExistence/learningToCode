This is American actor and comedian, Robin Williams. He brought a lot of laughter to a lot of people.

What is this?

This page came into being initially through FreeCodeCamp. I'm working through their Responsive Web Design curriculum, and this is the first of the five final projects.

Despite it being FreeCodeCamp that set me on this task, the topic itself is purely from my love of the actor.

Robin Williams, what can we say? He was an outstanding actor, a hilarious comedian and a towering inspiration to reach for the stars.

Let's celebrate some of his best loved movies and characters, as well as revisit some of his most touching quotes.

This is the life of Robin Williams. If you know who he was and loved his work - enjoy, and shed a smiley tear. If you don't know who he was... welcome, let's introduce you the right way!
